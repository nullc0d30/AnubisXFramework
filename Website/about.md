# About

**AnubisX Framework — Mission and Background**

---

## Mission

To establish a rigorous, reproducible scientific methodology for digital identity attribution, transforming it into a formal forensic discipline.

## Background

Digital attribution has long been a critical capability for cybersecurity, forensic investigation, and intelligence analysis. However, existing methods rely on technical indicators that sophisticated adversaries can easily circumvent. The AnubisX Framework was developed to address this fundamental gap by shifting the evidentiary foundation to human cognitive signatures.

## Approach

- **Scientific rigor**: All claims quantified, testable, and reproducible
- **Human-centric**: Focus on invariant operator characteristics
- **Multi-modal**: Convergence of independent signals for robust attribution
- **Ethical by design**: Privacy and proportionality embedded in architecture

## Status

The framework is a complete theoretical specification. Implementation and empirical validation are the next phase.

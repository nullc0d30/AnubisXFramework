## Value Proposition

Digital attribution today relies on transient technical artifacts that adversaries can change at will. The AnubisX Framework shifts the foundation from what adversaries use to who they are — their persistent, involuntary cognitive signatures.

## Key Messages

1. **Paradigm shift**: From technical to human-centric attribution
2. **Scientific discipline**: Establishing Identity Intelligence alongside DNA, fingerprints, ballistics
3. **Multi-modal**: Five independent behavioral signals fused for high-confidence attribution
4. **Quantified**: Every conclusion includes measured uncertainty
5. **Ethical**: Privacy, proportionality, and dual-use safeguards built in

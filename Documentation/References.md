## 3. External References (Required)

The following external works are foundational to the AnubisX Framework and should be consulted for deeper understanding:

### 3.1 Forensic Science

| Reference | Relevance |
|---|---|
| Aitken, C.G.G. & Taroni, F. (2004). Statistics and the Evaluation of Evidence for Forensic Scientists. Wiley. | LR framework, Bayesian reasoning |
| Evett, I.W. (1998). Towards a uniform framework for reporting opinions in forensic science casework. Science & Justice, 38(3), 198-202. | LR interpretation standards |
| Daubert v. Merrell Dow Pharmaceuticals, 509 U.S. 579 (1993). | US legal standard for scientific evidence admissibility |

### 3.2 Stylometry and Linguistics

| Reference | Relevance |
|---|---|
| Burrows, J.F. (2002). Delta: A measure of stylistic difference and a guide to likely authorship. Literary and Linguistic Computing, 17(3), 267-287. | Stylometric analysis methodology |
| Mosteller, F. & Wallace, D.L. (1964). Inference in an Authorship Problem. Journal of the American Statistical Association. | Foundational authorship attribution |
| Koppel, M., Schler, J., & Argamon, S. (2009). Computational methods in authorship attribution. Journal of the American Society for Information Science and Technology. | Modern stylometric methods |

### 3.3 Behavioral Biometrics

| Reference | Relevance |
|---|---|
| Jain, A.K., Ross, A., & Prabhakar, S. (2004). An introduction to biometric recognition. IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems for Video Technology, 14(1), 4-20. | Biometric framework and evaluation |
| Yampolskiy, R.V. & Govindaraju, V. (2008). Behavioural biometrics: a survey and classification. International Journal of Biometrics, 1(1), 81-113. | Behavioral biometric taxonomy |

### 3.4 Machine Learning and Signal Processing

| Reference | Relevance |
|---|---|
| Brummer, N. & du Preez, J. (2006). Application-independent evaluation of speaker detection. Computer Speech & Language, 20(2-3), 230-275. | C_lr evaluation metric |
| Shafer, G. (1976). A Mathematical Theory of Evidence. Princeton University Press. | Dempster-Shafer theory for evidence fusion |

### 3.5 Arabic NLP

| Reference | Relevance |
|---|---|
| Abdul-Mageed, M., et al. (2020). MARBERT: Deep Bidirectional Transformers for Arabic. arXiv:2101.01785. | MarBERT model for Arabic NLP |
| Antoun, W., et al. (2020). AraBERT: Transformer-based Model for Arabic Language Understanding. arXiv:2003.00104. | AraBERT model for Arabic NLP |

### 3.6 Cybersecurity and Attribution

| Reference | Relevance |
|---|---|
